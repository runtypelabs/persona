Our Services

From intimate gatherings to corporate events, we bring the warmth of our bakery to your special occasions.

Catering

Let us elevate your next event with our signature breads, pastries, and custom menus. From breakfast meetings to evening receptions, we create memorable spreads that bring people together.

  • Corporate breakfast & lunch packages
  • Private event bread service
  • Pastry platters for any occasion
  • Custom menus available

Starting at $15 per person • Minimum 10 guests

Custom Orders

Have a special request? Our bakers love a creative challenge. Whether it's a wedding cake, a celebration loaf, or something entirely unique, we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.

  • Wedding & celebration cakes
  • Specialty bread orders
  • Dietary accommodation options
  • Corporate gifting programs

Contact us for pricing • 2-week advance notice required

Bread Subscription

Never run out of fresh bread. Our weekly subscription delivers your favorite loaves straight to your door, still warm from the oven. Choose your frequency and selections, and we'll handle the rest.

  • Weekly or bi-weekly delivery
  • Choose your favorite loaves
  • Subscriber-only seasonal specials
  • Skip or pause anytime

Starting at $28/week • Free delivery on orders over $40

Baking Classes

Learn the art of bread making from our expert bakers. Our hands-on classes cover everything from starter cultivation to shaping techniques. Leave with your own loaf and the skills to recreate it at home.

  • Sourdough fundamentals (3 hours)
  • Croissant workshop (4 hours)
  • Private group sessions available
  • All materials included

$95-150 per person • Classes held on weekends
